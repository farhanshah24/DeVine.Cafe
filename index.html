Welcome to De Vine

Tucked beneath the historic railway arches of Vine Street, De Vine is a hidden gem in the heart of London’s bustling EC3 district. Just steps away from America Square, our restaurant offers a unique blend of atmosphere, character, and refined cuisine.
At De Vine, we celebrate bold flavours, seasonal ingredients, and elevated comfort food — all served in a stylish, contemporary setting. From expertly grilled mains to hand-crafted starters and curated wine selections, every detail is designed to delight.
Whether you're here for a quick lunch, an after-work unwind, or an intimate dinner, De Vine offers a relaxed, memorable experience rooted in taste and hospitality.
